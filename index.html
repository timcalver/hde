Staking, without a doubt, staking will be the heart of the project, the team will invest almost 30% of the total sales (about 1000 Sol) in the Liquidity pool, and add 50% of the royalties all weeks to the LP, yes it is a great promise and the community wants to make sure that we fulfill the terms, we contacted civic to be doxed, our biggest goal is to be the next blue chip project on the Solana blockchain, and we will do everything to become.

“Reback”, we want to attract flippers to our project as it is always good buying at an affordable price on the secondary market after sell out and it always gives more opportunity for new people to join the community. How do we have the possibility to present this % of the royalties for the “Reback” ? We can afford the Reback to our minters because we will not spend any money on advertisements, we truly believe that the project has great utility and a great team behind it and with hard work we will sell out and bring a great project for the community.

Breeding, breeding is also fundamental in the project as it will give numerous advantages when staking Gen 1, for example, when staking there will be 2 staking options 15 days or 30 days, for example whoever chooses the 15 day option will receive (1.0x) of tokens per day, by choosing 30 days you receive (1.3x). Whoever stake the dragon with the breeding will have an ability to untake when they want and receive (1.3x) like in the 30 day lock but with no lock. (The breeding will also go as a "dragon" when staking, will receive the same bonuses as a dragon). The breeding will be a WL only mint, whoever holds the dragons can mint via WL.

We will add 50% of royalties every week to the Liquidity Pool, the other 50% will go to the team.

Mythic Dragons will earn 7 times more then the normal dragons when staking, they will also get a free mint of the breeding with 95% legendary chance and 5% mythic. Mythic dragons can be unstaked at any time. 25% of the total sales value of Mythic Dragons will be added to the liquidity pool.
